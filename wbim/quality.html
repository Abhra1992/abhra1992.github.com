Assignment 3 - Information and Data Quality

Top 10 Criteria for Quality of Information on the Web

In my opinion, the following criteria are the top 10 (in order of importance) for determining the quality of information on the Web.

  • Availability + Accessibility + Openness
  • Authority + Provenance + Verifiability
  • Accuracy
  • Objectivity + Validity
  • Concise Representation + Composition
  • Timeliness
  • Scope of Coverage + Relevancy
  • Completeness + Documentation
  • Reliability + Reputation
  • Usability + Interpretability + Reproducibility
